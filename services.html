// Comprehensive hardware engineering solutions. From mixed-signal architectures to high-density interconnects.

01. SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

End-to-End Hardware Development

We engineer complex hardware ecosystems from architectural concept to mass production. Our team develops high-precision data acquisition units, robust power distribution networks, and custom automated test equipment (ATE) designed for manufacturing scalability.

02. ANALOG & MIXED-SIGNAL DESIGN

Low-Noise & High Precision

We specialize in high-fidelity analog front-ends and low-noise signal chains. Our expertise covers precision ADC/DAC integration and the development of stable current and voltage drivers required for sensitive instrumentation.

03. FPGA & LOGIC

RTL Design & Synthesis

We architect high-performance digital logic and FPGA systems. Using Verilog RTL, we design custom data acquisition pipelines and deterministic digital control loops optimized for real-time processing.

04. PCB Layout

High-Density & Signal Integrity

We execute complex multi-layer board layouts with a strict focus on signal integrity and thermal performance. Our designs prioritize mixed-signal isolation, efficient power delivery networks (PDN), and critical component placement for high-density applications.

05. DEBUG & FORENSICS

Root Cause Analysis

System-level troubleshooting and hardware validation. We identify bottlenecks in complex mixed-signal chains, resolve EMI/EMC compliance failures, and debug intermittent firmware/hardware race conditions using advanced logic analysis.
