About Me

Innovative Solutions Through System-Level Thinking

Connect with me on LinkedIn

I'm Baldeep Sanghera, an electrical engineer with a focus on analog and mixed-signal circuit design. I work at the intersection of hardware and systems—designing, building, and debugging everything from high-speed optoelectronic boards to power distribution systems and custom FPGA logic.

Over the past decade, I’ve worked across industries including telecommunications, photonics, and advanced manufacturing. My approach is hands-on and grounded: understand the system, design thoughtfully, and iterate quickly. Whether I’m bringing up a new board, reworking a tricky layout, or writing Verilog to debug a signal path, I’m most in my element solving real-world engineering problems.

I run my own consulting practice where I help teams move fast without compromising reliability. If you're developing custom electronics, working through a system design challange, or need support bringing up complex systems, I’d be glad to collaborate.
