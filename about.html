About Me

Innovative Solutions Through System-Level Thinking

Connect with me on LinkedIn

With over 12 years of experience designing everything from high-precision optical measurement systems to next-level FPGA solutions, I bring a knack for turning tough technical roadblocks into elegant, fully vetted hardware. I’ve tackled challenges across telecommunications, medical devices, test equipment, photonics, and beyond—so if it has a circuit, I’ve probably designed (or debugged) it. Above all, I’m here to deliver solutions that don’t just work, but work brilliantly.

I thrive on the thrill of wrangling complex problems, the kind that push the boundaries of what’s feasible in hardware design. Whether it’s nailing down a noisy analog circuit, developing a robust optoelectronic system, or orchestrating a power-savvy FPGA, I’m fueled by a mix of curiosity, creativity, and coffee. If you’ve got a circuit meltdown in progress, I’m the one rolling up my sleeves (and making sure the oscilloscope is warmed up).

My approach is as holistic as it is hands-on. I believe successful engineering marries technical brilliance with real-world pragmatism—considering manufacturability, reliability, and cost. From initial concept to final board layout, I keep one eye on the big picture and the other on the tiniest details, because in hardware design, the difference between “good” and “great” often comes down to growing trees to see a forest.

I believe in taking a holistic approach to engineering, considering not just the technical requirements but also practical aspects like manufacturability, reliability, and cost-effectiveness. This comprehensive perspective ensures that my solutions are not just technically sound but also commercially viable.
