My Lab & Technical Capabilities

I have a fully equipped electronics lab for rapid prototyping, testing, and debugging. My setup includes an oscilloscope, waveform generator, switching and linear power supplies, and a high-precision Keithley 2000 multimeter. I also have a 3D printer, metcal soldering iron, microscope, and the capability to solder 0201 components for high-density PCBs. For embedded systems development, I use a logic analyzer along with FPGA and microcontroller evaluation boards, allowing me to efficiently refine designs from concept to final product.
